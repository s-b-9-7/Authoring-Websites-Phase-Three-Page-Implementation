The Eastmont Community Center operates a food distribution program, providing services to a diverse group of clients. This program is dedicated to helping those facing food insecurity through donations. We serve minority, low-income family, and senior citizen residents within the 1st—supervisorial district through our distributions held every Friday between 8 am and 10 am.
